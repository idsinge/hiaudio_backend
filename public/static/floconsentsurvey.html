Participant Survey

I accept that Hi-AUDiO may process the data provided in this form solely for research purposes related to this pilot recording study of the Hi-Audio Online Platform.

I confirm that the information I'll provide is accurate to the best of my knowledge, and I agree that I may be contacted in the future for clarification or validation of my responses, if necessary.

All data collected during this pilot recording study will be treated confidentially and anonymised prior to publication or dataset release. Data will be analysed exclusively for academic and research publication purposes. No personally identifiable information will be disclosed in any publication, presentation, or dataset.

Participation in this study is entirely voluntary, and I understand that I may withdraw at any time, without providing a reason and without any negative consequences. If I choose to withdraw, all data associated with my participation will be deleted and excluded from further analysis. Participants may also request the deletion of their data at any time during the data retention period.

All data will be handled and stored in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable ethical guidelines. Data will be securely stored, accessible only to the research team, and will not be shared with third parties outside the research context. The data will be retained for a maximum period of one (1) year from the date of collection, after which it may be permanently deleted or anonymised.
